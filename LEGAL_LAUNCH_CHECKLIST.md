# FelaaAtelier Legal Launch Checklist

This checklist must be reviewed before any production release.

## Business Identity

- [ ] Confirm final trading name formatting
- [ ] Confirm legal business name if different from the public brand
- [ ] Confirm legal contact email
- [ ] Confirm business location wording used on legal pages
- [ ] Confirm copyright notice wording

## Policy Pages

- [ ] Privacy policy page published
- [ ] Terms page published
- [ ] Cookie policy page published
- [ ] Footer links to all legal pages are present on public routes
- [ ] Last-updated dates are set intentionally

## Tracking and Consent

- [ ] Analytics stays disabled or placeholder-only until approved
- [ ] Error tracking stays placeholder-only until approved
- [ ] Consent copy matches the tracking behavior actually implemented
- [ ] Cookie settings entry point exists if non-essential tracking is enabled

## Assets and Rights

- [ ] Image sources are owned, licensed, or approved for launch
- [ ] Fonts used in production are allowed for deployment
- [ ] Attribution requirements are satisfied where needed
- [ ] No export-era sample media remains unintentionally

## Compliance Review

- [ ] Legal copy reflects actual business practice
- [ ] Contact and data-use language matches the live implementation
- [ ] No unsupported claims are made about services, turnaround, or outcomes
- [ ] Accessibility review has been completed for public and legal routes
